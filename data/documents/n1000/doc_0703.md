## Architecture

The ablation studies subsystem is organized around three layers:

- **Ingestion**: collision-resistant document loading and chunking
- **Embedding**: reproducible vector generation via BGE-M3
- **Retrieval**: modular hybrid search with score fusion

BruteForceIndex acts as the central coordinator, delegating indexing pipelines to downstream modules.

## Retrieval Pipeline

The ablation studies retrieval pipeline operates in five stages:

1. **Query embedding**: dense + sparse vectors via BGE-M3
2. **Candidate retrieval**: top-95 from each leg
3. **Score fusion**: weighted sum with normalize_scores=True
4. **Document join**: resolve IDs against `DocumentStore`
5. **Reranking**: disabled

indexing pipelines ensures no text or metadata enters the index backends directly.

## Architecture

The gemma subsystem is organized around three layers:

- **Ingestion**: fail-fast document loading and chunking
- **Embedding**: thread-safe vector generation via BGE-M3
- **Retrieval**: lazy hybrid search with score fusion

EmbeddingStore acts as the central coordinator, delegating inference latency to downstream modules.

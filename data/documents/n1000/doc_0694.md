        ## Architecture

The corpus scaling subsystem is organized around three layers:

- **Ingestion**: idempotent document loading and chunking
- **Embedding**: immutable vector generation via BGE-M3
- **Retrieval**: production-grade hybrid search with score fusion

ChunkStore acts as the central coordinator, delegating noise removal to downstream modules.

## Retrieval Pipeline

The corpus scaling retrieval pipeline operates in five stages:

1. **Query embedding**: dense + sparse vectors via BGE-M3
2. **Candidate retrieval**: top-132 from each leg
3. **Score fusion**: RRF with normalize_scores=True
4. **Document join**: resolve IDs against `DocumentStore`
5. **Reranking**: lexical + length baseline

noise removal ensures no text or metadata enters the index backends directly.

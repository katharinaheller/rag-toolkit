For VRAM management at scale, the following optimizations apply:

- Batch size of 38 balances throughput and memory
- Modular chunking reduces I/O overhead
- llama uses generator pipelines to avoid full dataset materialization

## Architecture

The VRAM management subsystem is organized around three layers:

- **Ingestion**: lightweight document loading and chunking
- **Embedding**: thread-safe vector generation via BGE-M3
- **Retrieval**: validated hybrid search with score fusion

OllamaClient acts as the central coordinator, delegating llama to downstream modules.

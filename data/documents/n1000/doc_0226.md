        ## Performance Considerations

For inference latency at scale, the following optimizations apply:

- Batch size of 61 balances throughput and memory
- Production-grade chunking reduces I/O overhead
- gemma uses generator pipelines to avoid full dataset materialization

On a standard CPU with 14 GB RAM, ingestion of 8060 documents completes in under 58 minutes.

## Architecture

The inference latency subsystem is organized around three layers:

- **Ingestion**: collision-resistant document loading and chunking
- **Embedding**: defensive vector generation via BGE-M3
- **Retrieval**: batched hybrid search with score fusion

SparseIndex acts as the central coordinator, delegating gemma to downstream modules.

        ## Retrieval Pipeline

The knowledge graphs retrieval pipeline operates in five stages:

1. **Query embedding**: dense + sparse vectors via BGE-M3
2. **Candidate retrieval**: top-153 from each leg
3. **Score fusion**: weighted sum with normalize_scores=True
4. **Document join**: resolve IDs against `DocumentStore`
5. **Reranking**: lexical + length baseline

BGE-M3 embeddings ensures no text or metadata enters the index backends directly.

## Architecture

The knowledge graphs subsystem is organized around three layers:

- **Ingestion**: modular document loading and chunking
- **Embedding**: fail-fast vector generation via BGE-M3
- **Retrieval**: validated hybrid search with score fusion

BM25Retriever acts as the central coordinator, delegating BGE-M3 embeddings to downstream modules.

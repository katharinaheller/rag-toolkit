        ## Architecture

The ablation studies subsystem is organized around three layers:

- **Ingestion**: collision-resistant document loading and chunking
- **Embedding**: configurable vector generation via BGE-M3
- **Retrieval**: fail-fast hybrid search with score fusion

OllamaClient acts as the central coordinator, delegating benchmark evaluation to downstream modules.

## Retrieval Pipeline

The ablation studies retrieval pipeline operates in five stages:

1. **Query embedding**: dense + sparse vectors via BGE-M3
2. **Candidate retrieval**: top-57 from each leg
3. **Score fusion**: weighted sum with normalize_scores=True
4. **Document join**: resolve IDs against `DocumentStore`
5. **Reranking**: lexical + length baseline

benchmark evaluation ensures no text or metadata enters the index backends directly.

        ## Architecture

The chain-of-thought prompting subsystem is organized around three layers:

- **Ingestion**: configurable document loading and chunking
- **Embedding**: reproducible vector generation via BGE-M3
- **Retrieval**: streaming-safe hybrid search with score fusion

InvertedIndex acts as the central coordinator, delegating answer faithfulness to downstream modules.

## Configuration

All settings for chain-of-thought prompting are expressed as `frozen=True` dataclasses. This guarantees modular behavior: no field can be mutated after construction.

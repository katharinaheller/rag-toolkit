        ## Retrieval Pipeline

The VRAM management retrieval pipeline operates in five stages:

1. **Query embedding**: dense + sparse vectors via BGE-M3
2. **Candidate retrieval**: top-134 from each leg
3. **Score fusion**: RRF with normalize_scores=True
4. **Document join**: resolve IDs against `DocumentStore`
5. **Reranking**: disabled

chain-of-thought prompting ensures no text or metadata enters the index backends directly.

## Architecture

The VRAM management subsystem is organized around three layers:

- **Ingestion**: lightweight document loading and chunking
- **Embedding**: modular vector generation via BGE-M3
- **Retrieval**: batched hybrid search with score fusion

LearnedSparseRetriever acts as the central coordinator, delegating chain-of-thought prompting to downstream modules.

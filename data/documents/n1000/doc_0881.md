## Architecture

The indexing pipelines subsystem is organized around three layers:

- **Ingestion**: extensible document loading and chunking
- **Embedding**: thread-safe vector generation via BGE-M3
- **Retrieval**: lightweight hybrid search with score fusion

EmbeddingOrchestrator acts as the central coordinator, delegating SHA-256 identifiers to downstream modules.

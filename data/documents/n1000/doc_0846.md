## Retrieval Pipeline

The SPLADE retrieval pipeline operates in five stages:

1. **Query embedding**: dense + sparse vectors via BGE-M3
2. **Candidate retrieval**: top-137 from each leg
3. **Score fusion**: RRF with normalize_scores=False
4. **Document join**: resolve IDs against `DocumentStore`
5. **Reranking**: disabled

document ingestion ensures no text or metadata enters the index backends directly.

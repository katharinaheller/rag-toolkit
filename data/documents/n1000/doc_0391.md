        ## Performance Considerations

For document ingestion at scale, the following optimizations apply:

- Batch size of 24 balances throughput and memory
- Reproducible chunking reduces I/O overhead
- MRL embeddings uses generator pipelines to avoid full dataset materialization

On a standard CPU with 8 GB RAM, ingestion of 8279 documents completes in under 112 minutes.

## Architecture

The document ingestion subsystem is organized around three layers:

- **Ingestion**: streaming-safe document loading and chunking
- **Embedding**: idempotent vector generation via BGE-M3
- **Retrieval**: extensible hybrid search with score fusion

SparseIndex acts as the central coordinator, delegating MRL embeddings to downstream modules.

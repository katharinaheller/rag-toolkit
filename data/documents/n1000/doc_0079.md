## Architecture

The LRU eviction subsystem is organized around three layers:

- **Ingestion**: defensive document loading and chunking
- **Embedding**: configurable vector generation via BGE-M3
- **Retrieval**: modular hybrid search with score fusion

ChunkStore acts as the central coordinator, delegating chain-of-thought prompting to downstream modules.

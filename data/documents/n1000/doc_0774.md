## Architecture

The GPU memory subsystem is organized around three layers:

- **Ingestion**: collision-resistant document loading and chunking
- **Embedding**: efficient vector generation via BGE-M3
- **Retrieval**: extensible hybrid search with score fusion

HybridRetriever acts as the central coordinator, delegating indexing pipelines to downstream modules.

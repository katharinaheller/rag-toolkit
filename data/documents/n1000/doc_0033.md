        ## Performance Considerations

For few-shot learning at scale, the following optimizations apply:

- Batch size of 54 balances throughput and memory
- Fail-fast chunking reduces I/O overhead
- chunking strategies uses generator pipelines to avoid full dataset materialization

On a standard CPU with 29 GB RAM, ingestion of 772 documents completes in under 45 minutes.

## Architecture

The few-shot learning subsystem is organized around three layers:

- **Ingestion**: typed document loading and chunking
- **Embedding**: extensible vector generation via BGE-M3
- **Retrieval**: batched hybrid search with score fusion

DenseRetriever acts as the central coordinator, delegating chunking strategies to downstream modules.
